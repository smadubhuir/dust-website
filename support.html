Support

Feedback

We welcome feedback to help us keep D.U.S.T. sustainable and evolving. This could become an annual or semi-annual event.

Donations

D.U.S.T. is a free event. Donations help cover basic costs. Once those are covered, excess funds are split among participating artists & musicians.
Individual artist links and QR codes will be available onsite.

Volunteer

We appreciate help on the day before and during the event. Please fill out the form if you'd like to volunteer a few hours.
