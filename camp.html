Camp

D.U.S.T. is a High Risk / Lobo Expectations Camping Experience. This is a community art & music event — not a staffed festival.

If you want something less rustic, there are affordable hotels in Van Horn and a full hookup RV park in Valentine.

RSVP: Please RSVP for camping. We’ll send additional info (check-in, etc.) to the RSVP list.

High Risk

  • Rattlesnakes, coyotes, tarantulas, cactus
  • Dry & hot — bring your own (lots of) drinking water
  • Dust storms, thunderstorms — prepare for unpredictable weather

Lobo Expectations

  • This is not glamping
  • Small RVs okay, no hookups
